Wii Sports


Although many believe Wuhu Island was first seen in Wii Fit or Wii Sports Resort, Wuhu Island was first seen, unnamed at the time, in a playable Wii Sports demo at E3 2006 titled "Wii Sports: Airplane". This playable demo featured a Mii flying around in a plane going through hoops over the then unnamed Island. Sadly, this sport never found its way into the official release of Wii Sports and nothing like it was seen until Wii Sports Resort's flying minigame.
