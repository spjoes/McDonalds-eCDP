Wuhu Island


Wuhu Island is most commonly known from Wii Sports Resort, the first game to feature the island with its current/most-popular name. The first appearence of the island was in Wii Fit's jogging minigame where it was referred to as "Wii Fit Island". Since then, Wuhu Island has gotten several iterations and is seen several times throughout Nintendo games.
