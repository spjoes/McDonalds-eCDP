The McDonald's DS


The McDonald's DS was a matte-black DSi with a glossy black McDonald's logo on the top. This DS seemed to be packaged with the eCDP & eSmart games but seem to be exactly the same as a regular ds besides the custom shell. Since these DS' were distributed alongside eCDP and eSmart the public had very limited access to them.
